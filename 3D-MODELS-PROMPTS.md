# 3D Model Prompts for Stefany Fashion Website

Generate each model as a `.glb` file and place it in the `public/models/` folder.

---

## 1. `mannequin.glb`
**Prompt:** A stylish female fashion mannequin wearing an elegant long rose-gold evening gown with flowing silk fabric. The dress has a fitted bodice and a dramatic flowing skirt. The mannequin is faceless with a smooth head, standing in a graceful pose. Metallic rose-gold and champagne color palette. Low-poly stylized aesthetic suitable for web rendering.

---

## 2. `heart.glb`
**Prompt:** A glossy 3D heart shape, like a polished gemstone or crystal. Deep pink to rose-gold gradient color. Smooth reflective surface with slight transparency. Stylized and romantic, not anatomical. Small size, suitable as a floating decoration.

---

## 3. `diamond-ring.glb`
**Prompt:** An elegant diamond engagement ring with a thin gold band and a brilliant-cut diamond on top. The band is polished gold, the diamond is clear and sparkling white. Luxury jewelry style, clean and minimal. Small object, high detail on the gem facets.

---

## 4. `perfume-bottle.glb`
**Prompt:** A luxury perfume bottle with a rectangular glass body in translucent pink. Gold metallic cap on top. Minimalist and elegant design inspired by Italian luxury brands like Versace or Dolce & Gabbana. Clean lines, reflective glass material.

---

## 5. `rose.glb`
**Prompt:** A single beautiful red rose with a green stem. Stylized but recognizable petals in deep red and crimson. A few small leaves on the stem. Romantic and elegant, like a Valentine's Day gift rose. Suitable for floating animation in a 3D scene.

---

## 6. `high-heel.glb`
**Prompt:** A stylish women's high heel stiletto shoe in rose-gold or champagne metallic color. Sleek pointed toe, thin high heel. Luxury fashion design, clean and elegant. Shiny reflective material like patent leather or metallic finish.

---

## File Placement

After generating, place all files here:

```
public/
  models/
    mannequin.glb
    heart.glb
    diamond-ring.glb
    perfume-bottle.glb
    rose.glb
    high-heel.glb
```

Once the files are in place, let me know and I'll update the 3D scene to load them!
